Introduction

The NUTRIMEAL app caters to busy families seeking efficient meal planning solutions, helping them optimize their time and dietary differences while ensuring healthy eating habits. It addresses the challenges of coordinating family meals while meeting individual nutritional needs.

Project Vision:

To create an intuitive platform that simplifies meal planning, grocery shopping, and nutrition tracking for busy families, promoting healthier eating, food efficiency, and improved well-being across generations..

Problem Statement

Modern families often struggle to plan nutritious, balanced meals that meet the varying dietary needs of all members. Factors such as food allergies, age-specific nutrition, personal preferences, time constraints, and budget limitations make meal planning complex. There’s a lack of simple, accessible tools that can assist families in making optimized food choices without the need for multiple meals or extensive planning effort. Existing tools fail to address the nutritional complexities of multi-user households and they lack cultural adaptability, cross-generational tracking, and waste reduction features, leaving caregivers overwhelmed by fragmented solutions

Opportunity Identification

  • Lack of tools for family-wide meal planning
  • No apps addressing nutrition deficiencies
  • Poor integration with grocery APIs and inventory tracking
  • No intelligent assistants to help with real-time nutrition planning and smart substitutions

Market Opportunity

Indian online Food & Health Market Landscape (2024–2027) states that the Nutrition tech platforms like HealthifyMe & CureFit raised over ₹800 Cr combined in recent years, signaling growing investor interest.The healthy meal delivery market was ~₹3.1B in 2023 are expected to increase by ~₹7B+ by 2027.Meal planning/nutrition tracking apps globally see high user churn due to lack of personalization—opportunity for retention via AI and Indianized features.

The Nutrimeal Solution

NutriMeal focuses on solving core pain points in family meal planning by offering essential features like family profile management, simple meal and grocery planning, a basic nutrition dashboard, and AI-generated meal suggestions

Family Profiles

Track age, allergies, and preferences per member and save the knowledge base of family members

Daily/Weekly meal planner

Assign 3 time meals-breakfast, lunch, dinner to whole family, healthy and nutritional meals are generated through AI, in sync with grocery lists

Recipe Library

Filter by cuisine (North/South Indian), prep time, and diet

Integration with grocery apps

Options for connecting with plugin for grocery apps.Options to track inventory and purchases-either through physical/online store

Nutrition Dashboard

Visualize macros/micronutrients per meal; alert for gaps

AI Assistant

Easy resolution of your doubts
