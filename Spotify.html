Introduction

As music streaming becomes increasingly central to daily life, understanding how users interact with platforms like Spotify in their real-world contexts is crucial for creating meaningful user experiences.

Research Question:

How can we enhance Spotify's user experience by understanding the contextual behaviors, frustrations, and motivations of both free and premium users in the Indian market?

Through extensive contextual inquiry sessions, competitive analysis, and user journey mapping, our team of four researchers conducted an in-depth investigation into Spotify's user experience challenges and opportunities.

Research Methodology

Our research employed a mixed-methods approach, combining qualitative and quantitative techniques to gain comprehensive insights into user behavior and experience patterns.

Participant Demographics:

Our diverse sample included students, professionals, and homemakers across urban and rural areas, with ages ranging from 18-35 years. The mix included both free users (61%) and premium subscribers (39%).

Problem Statements

Through our research, we identified two critical problems that significantly impact user satisfaction and engagement with Spotify.

Problem 1: Discovery Fatigue

Discovery Fatigue and Over-Familiarity with Recommendations

Regular Spotify users struggle with a lack of freshness and relevance in music discovery. Algorithmic playlists often feel repetitive, predictable, or disconnected from users' evolving preferences.

"It becomes so predictable‚ÄîI already know which song is going to play next." ‚Äî Malavika

Impact: Decreased engagement with algorithmic content and undermines Spotify's key value proposition of intelligent music discovery.

Problem 2: Hidden Features

Hidden and Unused Features Undermining User Control

Users are unaware of or unable to find advanced features like the Equalizer. The home screen design is perceived as cluttered and overwhelming.

"I didn't even know there was an equaliser. If it's there, it's really hard to find." ‚Äî Nithin

Impact: Affects satisfaction and retention as users perceive the product as bloated or poorly designed.

Contextual Summary Insights

Our contextual inquiry revealed fascinating patterns in how users integrate Spotify into their daily lives, uncovering both expected and surprising behaviors.

üéß Key Behavioral Patterns

Passive Listening Dominance:

Most participants used Spotify during routine activities like cooking, working, or commuting rather than active listening sessions.

Device Flexibility:

While mobile apps were prioritized, users seamlessly switched between devices based on context (laptop for work, TV for cooking, phone for personal time).

Solo Preference:

All participants preferred listening alone, with social features used passively rather than actively.

External Discovery:

Users increasingly rely on friends, Instagram reels, and YouTube for music discovery rather than Spotify's algorithms.

Critical Finding:

Ads and skip restrictions were the primary motivators for premium upgrades‚Äînot feature richness. Users upgrade to remove friction, not to gain capabilities.

"I used to use the cracked APK, but it kept failing. When the student discount came in, it was just easier to pay. The free version was so annoying with back-to-back ads." ‚Äî Nithin

üåè Regional Music Challenges

Script Barrier

Users need lyrics in Roman script or English for regional language songs they can't read in native scripts.

Cultural Preference

Strong demand for Malayalam, Tamil, Gujarati, Punjabi, and Hindi music with better lyrical support.

Accuracy Issues

Existing translated lyrics often contain errors, reducing user trust and engagement.

Conclusions

This comprehensive UX research project revealed significant opportunities for Spotify to enhance user satisfaction and competitive positioning in the Indian market.

üéØ Key Recommendations

Reinvent Discovery:

Implement user-controllable serendipity features and reduce algorithmic predictability through genre mixing and temporal variety

Surface Hidden Value:

Make advanced features like equalizer more discoverable and provide contextual onboarding for power users

Context-Aware Interface:

Develop Work Mode and other contextual interfaces that adapt to different listening scenarios

Regional Localization:

Improve lyrics support for Indian languages with accurate transliteration and script switching
