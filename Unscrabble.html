Overview

UNSCRABBLE is a digitization-themed training game designed to enhance digital literacy and cognitive skills among intellectually disabled adults. Developed during a user-centered design project at AMBA (Bangalore), the game was inspired by the real-world challenges faced by this community in engaging with modern digital platforms and tools.

Core Mission:

Creating an inclusive, gamified learning experience that bridges the digital divide for intellectually disabled adults through tactile, visual, and auditory engagement.

Problem Statement

Individuals with intellectual disabilities often face barriers when trying to navigate basic digital interfaces. Training programs often lack engagement and accessibility tailored to their cognitive needs.

Key Challenges

Cognitive Barriers

Complex interfaces overwhelm users with intellectual disabilities

Engagement Issues

Traditional training methods lack interactive and motivating elements

Accessibility Gaps

Existing digital literacy programs not designed for diverse cognitive abilities

Feature Suggestions

Game

Unscrabble is a word formation game inspired by the mechanics of word puzzles. It was designed to develop spelling and recognition skills through simple, rewarding tasks.The aim was to combine learning with enjoyment, allowing users to play and practice without feeling pressure or frustration.

Core Gameplay

Tile Unscrambling

Players unscramble tiles to form commonly seen icons (home, settings, search)

Progressive Difficulty

Increasing levels of complexity with supportive nudges and hints

Reinforcement Learning

Identification

Separate cards with letters and numbers that can rearranged infinitely,also it is tactile

Memorize

Learning
