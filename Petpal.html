Introduction

PetPal is a comprehensive digital platform designed to revolutionize pet care by consolidating all essential pet services into one convenient, user-friendly application. It addresses the challenges faced by urban pet owners in accessing quality, reliable pet care services.

Project Vision:

To create a one-stop solution that provides pet parents with everything they need for their furry family members - from healthcare and grooming to nutrition and boarding - accessible at the tap of a finger.

Problem Statement

Pet parents in urban India struggle to access consolidated, trustworthy services for their pets—ranging from healthcare and grooming to nutrition and staycare. Existing services are fragmented, leading to inconvenience, lack of timely care, and inadequate information for pet well-being.

Current Challenges

  • Using multiple apps and websites for different pet services
  • Uncertainty about service quality and safety during boarding
  • Missed vaccination schedules due to lack of reminders
  • Difficulty finding vetted pet care providers

Market Opportunity

India's pet care market was worth ₹29,000 Cr in 2024 (~$3.5B) and is growing at 16-18% annually. Urban pet parents increasingly seek convenience, quality, and digital-first pet care experiences.

Research Insight:

Through user interviews and surveys, we identified that 78% of pet owners would prefer a single platform for all pet care needs, and 65% have missed important pet care milestones due to lack of reminders.

The PetPal Solution

PetPal is a one-stop digital platform offering a full suite of services for pet care, designed to address all the pain points identified in our user research.

Vet Services

Online consultations, clinic visits, vaccination reminders, and healthcare packs with insurance options.

Grooming

Customizable grooming services with certified professionals, transparent pricing, and at-home options.

Boarding

Vetted staycations and pet hotels with real-time updates and safety verification.

Pet Mart

Curated selection of food, toys, and accessories with subscription options.

Nutrition

Custom nutrition plans based on breed, age, and health conditions.

AI Assistant

24/7 chatbot for pet care advice, medication reminders, and behavioral tips.

Minimum Viable Product

MVP Features

  • User registration & pet profile setup
  • Booking for vet consultations and grooming services
  • Shopping module with limited inventory
  • Push notifications for health check reminders
  • Payment integration for booking and purchases
  • Basic chatbot support for FAQs

Revenue Model

  • Service commission (15-25% per booking)
  • Premium subscription for advanced features
  • E-commerce margins on product sales
  • Affiliate marketing with pet food brands
  • Ad space for related services

UX Design

Designed using Figma, the UX flow prioritizes holistic pet care services with an intuitive interface that makes all features easily discoverable.
