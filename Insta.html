FOCUS

ANALYSIS

Thematic Coding

Introduction

Instagram has emerged as a powerful social commerce platform by combining the appeal of visual content with seamless e-commerce functionality. With over 2.5 billion users globally, its features—like shoppable posts, influencer-driven campaigns, and direct purchasing—enable users to discover and buy products within a single interface.

Research Purpose:

This study explores how users interact with Instagram for e-commerce and evaluates their experiences and concerns in the digital marketplace, providing insights into social commerce behaviors and user trust factors.

Methodology

This qualitative study used semi-structured interviews with 22 Instagram users, selected across various shopping behaviors and demographics to ensure comprehensive insights into social commerce patterns.

Participant Selection

  • 22 Instagram users with varied shopping behaviors
  • Diverse demographics and purchasing patterns
  • Regular social media and e-commerce users

Research Focus

  • Instagram features: reels, posts, stories
  • DM-based purchases and transactions
  • Product discovery and trust factors

Analysis Approach:

Themes were identified through systematic coding, focusing on product discovery, transaction satisfaction, and trust concerns. Ethical standards including anonymity and informed consent were strictly followed.

Key Insights:

Participants valued personalized feeds and influencer recommendations for fashion, beauty, and decor items. However, concerns about DM-based shopping transparency and refund processes indicate areas for platform improvement.

Conclusion

Instagram offers a visually rich and engaging platform for social commerce, effectively capturing user attention and driving product discovery. However, trust and security concerns present opportunities for platform enhancement.

Key Recommendations

Enhance Trust

Implement a comprehensive verification system for sellers to build user trust and reduce concerns about fake accounts and fraudulent transactions and implement reviewing systems

Streamline Shopping

Add a dedicated e-commerce section for each small business for smoother shopping and transaction, delivery tracking

Better Delivery and Support

Improve timelines and seller responsiveness to foster buyer loyalty

Robust after sales policies

To address disputes and returns

Final Assessment:

For Instagram to become a trusted and complete e-commerce solution, implementing verified seller systems, robust customer support, and secure transaction processes will align user expectations with platform capabilities and foster long-term loyalty in social commerce.
